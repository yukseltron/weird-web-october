The Hidden Man

They say he has four eyes. Two to see the present, one to see the future, and one to see the past.

They say he grants wishes in return for sacrifices. Anything in the world in return for the flesh of your loved ones. Your heart has to be broken.

They say he was found, in some valley some place no one cares about. Too far to cause trouble except for what few people came around.

They say things might've changed. The air feels different around here. Like someone set him free to torment this place. And none of us understood.
